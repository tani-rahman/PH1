Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Leverage AI for personalization, embrace sustainable practices, optimize operations with automation, adopt omnichannel marketing, prioritize customer experience, foster agility, build community partnerships, focus on niche markets, and encourage employee innovation.

Sustainability

Highlight environmental initiatives, carbon footprint reduction, waste management, energy efficiency, sustainable sourcing, community impact, ethical practices,and future goals to demonstrate commitment to long-term environmental and social responsibility.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure encompasses physical and organizational systems, including transportation, communication networks, and facilities, essential for economic development, societal functioning, and improving quality of life, fostering resilience, connectivity, and sustainable growth.

Empowerment

Empowerment is fostering confidence, autonomy, and capability in individuals or groups by providing resources, education, opportunities, and support, enabling them to make informed decisions and achieve their full potential.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation in Bangladesh accelerates innovation, boosts economic growth, and enhances public services through e-governance, fintech, digital education, and smart infrastructure, driving inclusivity and fostering a tech-driven, sustainable future.

Green Energy

Green energy utilizes renewable sources like solar, wind, and hydro to produce sustainable, eco-friendly power, reducing carbon emissions, combating climate change, promoting energy security, and ensuring a cleaner, healthier planet.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure integrates advanced technology, smart systems, and sustainable practices, supporting efficient transportation, energy, communication, and urban development to enhance connectivity, resilience, and quality of life for communities.

In a defining moment of our history, Bangladesh is undergoing a monumental transformation. After more than 15 years under a regime marked by corruption, oppression and exploitation, a courageous revolution led by our students has ignited a new era. The fall of this autocratic government has paved the way for an unprecedented opportunity to rebuild our beloved nation under the interim leadership of Dr. Muhammad Yunus—Nobel laureate, visionary economist, and a beacon of hope. This new chapter, "Bangladesh 2.0," represents not just a political shift, but the rekindling of our national spirit.

Bangladesh stands before a transformative political phase, driven by a people's call for a drastic paradigm shift in politics. The people have collectively emerged as a new political community—one that transcends traditional notions of the nation-state. This de novo political community is not a fleeting product of the 36 days of July but rather the culmination of years of sustained political resistance.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
